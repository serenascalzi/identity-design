Girls Who Code College Loops are on-campus programs for female-identifying and non-binary college students. In College Loops, women hone their computer science skills and engage with other women to take action, collaborate, create, and inspire one another!

The program includes workshops, exciting monthly computer science challenges and exclusive professional development opportunities with industry professionals and Girls Who Code corporate partners.
